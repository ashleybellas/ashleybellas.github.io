Hello and Welcome!


Thank you for visiting my homepage. Here you'll find the research related to my PhD in Geophysics. I specialize in computational geodynamics with particular interest in lithospheric dynamics, rheology, and flexure. I am now a postdoctoral fellow at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, where I am exploring analytic techniques relevant to the dynamics and structure of Earth's lower mantle.


The fundamental questions that drive my research include: what is the nature of the lithospheric rheology that gives rise to the unique tectonic style of Earth? Are the many methods and models used to constrain rheology consistent with each other, and do they simulate real Earth systems adequately? If not, how can they be reconciled or improved?


I am highly interested in making connections with scientists of similar and dissimilar expertise, so I encourage you to reach out if you see a potential for us to collaborate!
